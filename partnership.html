Growing Partnership Program

What This Is About

We believe in supporting passionate growers who are doing meaningful work but might need resources to reach their full potential. This isn't about formal contracts or strict obligations - it's about investing in people and projects we believe in.

How It Works

Your Investment: Your passion, growing space, time, and whatever projects you're already working on.

Our Investment: Practical support - equipment, supplies, genetics, emergency funds, and consulting when needed.

The Partnership: You continue developing your growing skills and projects, we provide the resources to help you succeed.
